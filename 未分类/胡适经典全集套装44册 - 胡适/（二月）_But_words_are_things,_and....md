# （二月）_But_words_are_things,_and...

二三、裴伦论文字之力量

（二月）

But words are things, and a small drop of ink,

Falling like dew upon a thiught, produces

That which makes thousands, perhaps millions, think.

—Byron,

Don Juan

, Canto Ⅲ, St. 88.

〔

中译

〕

语言是看得见的东西，一滴墨水

像露珠滴在思想之上，

它使无数人展开了思维的翅膀。

——裴伦：《唐璜》
