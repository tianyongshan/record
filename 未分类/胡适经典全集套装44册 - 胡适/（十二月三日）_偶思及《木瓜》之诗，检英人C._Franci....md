四七、译《诗经·木瓜》诗一章

（十二月三日）

偶思及《木瓜》之诗，检英人C. Francis Romilly Allen所译观之，殊未惬心，因译之如下：

投我以木祧，报之以琼瑶.

匪报也，永以为好也。

Peaches were the gifts which to me you made,

And I gave you back a piece of jade—

Not to compensate

Your kindnesses, my friend,

But to celebrate

Our friendship which shall never end.
